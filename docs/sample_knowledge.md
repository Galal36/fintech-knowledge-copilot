# Merchant Pricing Review - April 2026

## Weekly Pricing Decisions

- The pricing team approved a pilot for premium merchant bundles at 2.9% + 3 EGP per transaction for low-churn merchants.
- Standard merchants remain on 3.2% + 3 EGP per transaction until the pilot results are reviewed.
- Enterprise merchants negotiating annual volume commitments can receive a reduced rate of 2.5% + 2 EGP per transaction.

## Support Feedback

- Support reported repeated complaints that merchants do not understand the difference between settlement fees and transaction fees.
- Refund policy confusion increased after the April fee update email because the email did not clearly state that refund processing fees remain unchanged.

## Risk Notes

- Finance requested that all pricing communication include explicit language that chargeback handling fees are not part of the premium bundle pilot.
- Product noted that inconsistent language across Slack and meeting notes could create merchant confusion if rollout messaging is not centralized.

## Decision Summary

- Run the premium bundle pilot for two weeks.
- Measure churn, transaction volume, and support ticket volume before expanding the pricing change.
